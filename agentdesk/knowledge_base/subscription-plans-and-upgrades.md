# Subscription Plans and Upgrades

**Summary:**
An overview of TeamFlow's subscription tiers, feature differences, how to upgrade or downgrade your plan, and what happens at cancellation.

**Tags:** subscription, plans, upgrade, downgrade, free, pro, business, enterprise, pricing, tier, cancel, renewal

## Overview

TeamFlow offers four subscription tiers designed to scale with your team. Each plan unlocks additional features, seat capacity, and support levels.

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**.
2. Select the desired tier.
3. Confirm the pricing change — upgrades take effect **immediately**.
4. You will be charged a **prorated amount** for the remainder of your current billing period.

> Upgrading unlocks new features instantly. No workspace restart is required.

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**.
2. Select a lower tier.
3. Confirm the downgrade.

Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing period**. Until then, you retain access to your current plan's features.

> **Important:** If your team size exceeds the seat limit of the lower tier, you must remove users before the downgrade takes effect, or the downgrade will be blocked.

---

## Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan**.
2. Select a cancellation reason (optional).
3. Confirm cancellation.

Your workspace will remain active until the end of your paid period. After that, the workspace is downgraded to the **Free tier**. Data is retained for **90 days** before permanent deletion if you do not re-subscribe.

---

## Auto-Renewal

All plans renew automatically by default. To disable auto-renewal:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Auto-Renewal**.
2. Toggle **Auto-Renew** to off.

You will receive a reminder email **14 days before** your plan expires if auto-renewal is disabled.

Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager and custom onboarding support.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I mix monthly and annual billing for different seats?**
No. The billing cycle applies to the entire workspace. All seats fall under the same plan and billing frequency.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
Your data is retained for 90 days post-cancellation. You can re-subscribe at any time within this window to restore full access.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription instead of cancelling?**
Subscription pausing is not currently available. You can cancel and re-subscribe at any time without losing your data within the 90-day window.
